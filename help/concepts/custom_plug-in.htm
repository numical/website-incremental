Custom Plug-In

Epic has been designed to allow easy and rapid customisation to best fit your working practices. If Epic's preferences do not offer enough flexibility, you can develop a custom plug-in for your company.

As explained in plug-ins, Epic's functionality is the sum of individual plug-ins installed on your computer. A custom plug-in is simply another plug-in that has been written to extend or enhance Epic's base functionality. In several key areas Epic has been written expecting such extensions - hence adding them can be very rapid. Such areas include, but are not limited to:

  • all import functions, e.g.:
    • import estimate tasks
    • manhour entry
  • status changes of:
    • projects
    • purchase items
  • code generation
  • document exports
  • task alerts

The custom plug-in can alos be used to override the text Epic displays.
