Employee

An employee is any person working for your company.

Epic can store all contact information that a contact would have plus additional employment-specific information.

As employee is a categorised record.

As many yards have separate systems for personnel management Epic can run without employee data being entered. In this case the manhour entry process may use a custom manhour import process provided by a custom plug-in.
