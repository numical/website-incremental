Create a New Product / Vessel

Activity required : shiprepair.marketing.rw
Plug-in required : Marketing

A new product record can be created in one of three ways:

  • during the create enquiry or create contract process
  • whilst creating a new association
  • directly:

This page details how to create a product directly

  1. Switch to the Marketing plug-in;
  2. Ensure the 'products' option on the search criteria is selected and click the new product button:

  3. A product editor will open to allow you to add details:
    • only the product's name is required
    • the product record is not created until the editor is first saved
